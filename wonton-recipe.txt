Ingredients:
-cream cheese
-wonton wrappers
-water
-oil for cooking

Directions:

1. Lay wrappers flat
2. Spoon 1/2 tablespoon cream cheese into center of each wrapper
3. Wet wrapper edges with water, fold into triangle, press edges together, use fancy folds if desired
4. Heat a few tablespoons of oil in pan, fry wontons until both sides are golden brown
